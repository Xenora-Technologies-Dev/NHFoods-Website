Our Story

Premium Quality Since Day One

NH Foods operates under NAJM ALHUDA FOODSTUFF TRADING LLC, a Dubai-based food trading company and trusted food supplier in Dubai. We specialize in supplying premium meat, poultry, seafood and dairy products to hotels, restaurants and catering businesses across the UAE.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between world-class food producers and the UAE's demanding hospitality sector. Today, we're proud to serve leading hotels, restaurants, and catering companies with consistent quality and reliable service.

We understand that in the food business, quality and timing are everything. That's why we've invested in state-of-the-art cold chain logistics and built relationships with trusted suppliers worldwide.
